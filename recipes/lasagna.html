~LASAGNA~

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, creamy b√©chamel sauce, cheese, and often a savory filling such as ground meat or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or pre-cooked according to package instructions)
  • 2 cups (about 500g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (about 250g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1cup (about 250g) ricotta cheese
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
