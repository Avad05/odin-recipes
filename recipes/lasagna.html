LASAGNA

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish known for its layers of pasta sheets, rich tomato sauce, creamy béchamel sauce, cheese, and often a savory filling such as ground meat or vegetables.

To assemble lasagna, the ingredients are layered in a baking dish, starting with a thin layer of tomato sauce, followed by pasta sheets, béchamel sauce, cheese, and the filling. This layering process is repeated until the dish is full,finishing with a top layer of sauce and cheese. It's then baked in the oven until the pasta is tender and the cheese is melted and bubbly. Lasagna is typically served hot and can be accompanied by a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal. Its comforting flavors and hearty layers make it a beloved dish around the world.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles (oven-ready or pre-cooked according to package instructions)
  • 2 cups (about 500g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup (about 250g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1cup (about 250g) ricotta cheese
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
